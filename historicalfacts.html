HISTORICAL FACTS

Teenage pregnancy which is also known as "adolescent pregnancy" is when a female under the age of 20 gets pregnant. The pregnancy can occur with sexual intercourse after the start of ovulation which can be before the first menstrual period but usually occurs after the onset of periods. In well-nourished females, the first period usually takes place around the age of 12 or 13. Many pregnant teenagers face the same pregnancy related issues as other grown woman but there is additional concerns for those under the age 15 because they are less likely to be physically developed. Due to this they are less likely to sustain a healthy pregnancy or to give birth.

TEEN PREGNANCY: THE SIGNS

Missing one or more menstrual period is a obvious sign of pregnancy, Nausea or vomitting which in this case is called "morning sickness", unusual fatigue, unusual mood swings or sudden digust in food.
